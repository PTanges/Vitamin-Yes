Folate is an important part of your diet

Hello {{studentName}}, did you know? A general term for the various forms of folic acid, a B vitamin. Folate is needed to make DNA, RNA, and amino acids.
It occurs naturally in foods and is found in leafy green vegetables (such as spinach and turnip greens), fruits (such as citrus fruits and juices), and dried beans and peas.
